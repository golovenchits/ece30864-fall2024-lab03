a.  Pros: fast and efficient development, keep track of all issues. 
    Cons: requires special training to use jira, need to hire jira manager to handle scrums

b.  Product Owner – Sets direction for the team, handles release management and stakeholder management.
    Scrum Master - All scrum related tasks – JIRA tooling, facilitating sprints and meetings, standups all done by the Scrum Master

c.  spring planning: Determine what to complete in the coming sprint, Move stories in sprint scope from backlog to current sprint
    daily scrum: Team members discuss work completed, work plan for the day and any blockers
    sprint retrospective: Discuss work complete and move incomplete work to backlog

d. 
  epic - Captures a large body of work that can be broken down into several smaller stories
  Story -  A software system requirement that is expressed in a few short sentences.
  bug - smallest unit of work, can block stories
  task - step in a process
